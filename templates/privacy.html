Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account information — your email address and hashed password when you register.
  • Expense data — the amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter manually.
  • Usage data — basic request logs (page visited, timestamp) for debugging and reliability purposes.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data from your device beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate the Service for you:

  • To authenticate your account and keep your session secure.
  • To store and display your expense records.
  • To generate spending summaries and category breakdowns shown in the app.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond running your personal expense tracker.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in a secured database accessible only to your account. Passwords are hashed before storage and are never readable in plain text — not even by us.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is permanently removed from our systems.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with any third parties. We do not integrate advertising networks, analytics platforms, or data brokers.

The only external resource loaded by the app is Google Fonts (for typography). Google's own privacy policy governs any data collected through that request.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, you can reach us through the contact details provided within the application.

We will respond to all privacy-related inquiries within a reasonable timeframe.
